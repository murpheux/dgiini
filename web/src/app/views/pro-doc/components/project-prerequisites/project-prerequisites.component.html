4. PRIOR OBLIGATIONS
The following elements will be required prior to the commencement of the project:
4.1. Signature of project document by both parties.
4.2. The required funding to cover the cost of the project. The project will be considered as operationally active only upon acknowledgment of receipt of funds by ICAO
4.3. The assignment of a National Coordinator/focal point by the State and assignment of counterparts, as required.
4.4. Preparation and/or submission of the necessary documents to ensure the ICAO expert(s) may commence project activities upon the start of their assignment(s).
4.5. Preparation of authorizations required for access to work sites by the ICAO expert(s).
