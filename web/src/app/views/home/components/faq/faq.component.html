Frequently Asked Questions?

How do I get started?

You can get started by creating a profile as a tasker, a client or both. on the platform. By creating your profile, you have full access to all the benefits the platform has to offer. As a registered client, you can post and schedule tasks while as a tasker, you can bid for tasks and get paid instantly once the tasks are completed.

Are taskers vetted before being registered

All our taskers are vetted by running their profiles though the provincial and federal police database. Furthermore, taskers in any of the professional/ regulated fields are required to submit their qualifying certificates. All taskers are also required to provide a minimum of 2 copies of government issued ID as proof of identification and residency.

How do I know which tasker is qualified to perform specific tasks?

Can I negotiate offers privately

We strongly discourage negotiating offers and acceptance outside the platform. All chats between the tasker and the client can be done via the chat section of the platform and these communications are secured and are only seen by the tasker and the client. By keeping restricting all communication to the platform, it makes it easy for all parties to resolve any misunderstandings.

How much does the service cost

The cost of each task varies. The Client stipulates how much he/ she wants to pay for tasks and once the task is posted, taskers are able to bid based on their availability and skills level and then, the client can select his/ her preferred tasker. Therefore, the cost of each task is determine by both the client and the tasker
